# Lead Rescue Admin OS Playbook

## Positioning

Lead Rescue Admin OS is a practical admin automation package for existing small businesses that quote jobs manually and lose money through slow replies, weak follow-up, scattered inboxes, and unpaid balances.

Primary business types:

1. Trades and repair services
2. Rentals and event hire
3. Restoration and upholstery
4. Cleaning and landscaping
5. Security installers
6. Builders and maintenance teams
7. Local venues and service operators

## The Lead Rescue Rule

Every enquiry must end with one of these next actions:

1. Customer sends photos
2. Customer sends measurements
3. Customer sends location or site details
4. Owner books a call or site visit
5. Customer receives a quote
6. Lead is marked not-fit or manual review

No enquiry should sit as "will get back to you" without a next step.

## Fast Reply Template

Hi [Name], thanks for contacting us. We can help with [job type] in your area.

To quote properly, please send:

- Clear photos or video
- Rough measurements
- Your suburb or location
- Any style, timing, or access details

Once I have that, I can advise whether we can quote from photos or arrange a site visit.

## Quote Evidence Checklist

For install or repair jobs:

- Close-up and wide-angle photos
- Width, height, or rough size
- What failed or what needs to be built
- Material or surface type if known
- Site access and parking
- Urgency and deadline

For custom work:

- Reference image or sketch
- Approximate dimensions
- Indoor or outdoor use
- Functional or decorative requirement
- Finish preference
- Target budget and deadline

## Seven-Day Revenue Recovery Sprint

Day 1: Audit existing WhatsApp, Facebook, email, phone, and referral enquiries for missed follow-ups.

Day 2: Update the strongest service offer and intake questions.

Day 3: Reactivate old warm leads and quotes before doing cold outreach.

Day 4: Post one focused service offer with direct enquiry CTA.

Day 5: Contact 20 partner/referral targets.

Day 6: Follow up every warm lead and push for quotes, site visits, or deposits.

Day 7: Review recovered leads, new enquiries, booked site visits, quoted value, paid deposits, and jobs won.

## Daily Owner Summary

At the end of each day, report:

1. Hot leads needing action
2. Leads waiting for photos or measurements
3. Site visits to book
4. Quotes waiting for approval
5. Deposits or balances to chase
6. Completed jobs needing review requests
